FAQs(Frequently asked questions)

  • Account Related

    • 1. How do i create an account ?

    Go to the Sign Up page, enter your Name, Email, and Password, then click "Create Account".

    2. How can I reset my password?

    Click on "Forgot Password" on the Login page. Enter your registered email and follow the link sent to your inbox.

  • Budget Planner Usage

    • 3. How do I add my income?

    Login to your account → Go to Dashboard → Click "Add Income" → Enter details → Save.

    4. How do I track my expenses?

    Go to Dashboard → Click "Add Expense" → Enter Amount, Category, and Date → Save.

    5. Can I set savings goals?

    Yes, go to the "Savings Goal" section in your dashboard, set your target amount, and track your progress.
